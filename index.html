Weatherford
Our global careers are powered by growth and innovation. We have a place for experienced energy industry professionals, up-and-coming NextGen talent, and military veterans. We hire at all levels of opportunity for roles out in the field, in manufacturing facilities, in the office, and at operating sites in 75 countries. Wherever you go with Weatherford, you’ll collaborate with world-class experts who will inspire your best and most purposeful work.We deliver advancements that propel our Company, industry, and customers forward. Weatherford’s dynamic and exciting environment is powered by possibility as we transform the energy industry. Our innovative solutions integrate proven technologies with advanced digitalization to create environmentally and economically sustainable offerings.People are at the center of our One Weatherford culture. We are united by a shared purpose across all geographies, cultures, languages, and career areas. Working here means belonging to a friendly and inclusive team that readily shares knowledge and communicates openly. From Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) to regular Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DE&I) training, you’ll have the tools to thrive in our supportive and trusting workplace.We want to support you in your journey to leadership. NextGen is a global Leadership Development Program (LDP) for Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Petroleum Engineering, and Chemical Engineering graduates with a Bachelor of Science degree. The program will accelerate your growth and prepare you for a leadership role on our responsive global team.
